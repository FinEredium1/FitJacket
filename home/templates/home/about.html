About

FitJacket is a fitness app made by students to encourage people to live a healthy lifestyle. Fitjacket is packed with lots of features to get you working out. By signing up for FitJacket, you will receive exclusive access to an AI training/coaching assistant that will be able to work with you along your journey! Furthermore, as a FitJacket user, you will be able to connect with others to compete in different fitness goals/challenges.

Discover the convenience of our digital platform, where you get on-track to living a healthy lifestyle!
